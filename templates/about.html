ABOUT OUR COMPANY
Rooted in Values of Quality, Tradition and Innovation
Westar is committed to developing elite vegetable genetics which through intellectual rigor, tireless effort and an organized vision express the quality that the brand stands for. We combine traditional values and service with innovative techniques to transform the highest quality genetics into the best vegetable seed varieties to serve professional growers.

Headquartered in California’s Imperial Valley, Westar honorably serves customers in over 50 countries by our dedicated, professional staff. Our respect for the agricultural industry and responsibility we have to continue to contribute to the global food supply has been the forefront of our mandate ever since our inception in 1992.

Our mission
We maintain our integrity throughout all our business practices and day-to-day operations. We believe integrity is the foundation on which we’re built and grow upon. Nothing is a higher priority than building strong relationships with our customers and partners. At Westar, our customers become friends and together we grow a bright future. We dont’t limit ourselves to quality in seed, plants, and final product only. we consider quality in customer service, business, and efficiency in serving you as absolutely essential

OUR GOAL WAS NEVER TO BE THE BIGGEST, BUT TO ALWAYS BE THE BEST
In 1992, after two decades of rising through the ranks within multiple seed companies, John Sonza founded Westar Seeds International, Inc. in El Centro, California. From those first days, the company was guided by his determination to be the best, not the biggest. The key was in delivering more with every order and for every customer: more expertise, more support, and more yield.

Superior customer service, John believed, is what would set Westar Seeds apart. He was right, and years of consistent growth followed. Westar Seeds’ unique customer-centric approach can be seen in every employee’s friendly, service-oriented nature. The team earns each customer’s trust the old-fashioned way, never forgetting they have a choice and that a promise made must be a promise kept.
