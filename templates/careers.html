Come Join Our Team
Join one of the greatest ensembles of experts and employees in either the international or domestic seed business. From hand work directly with plants in the field to seed handling, shipping, and working with customers from all over the globe, there’s bound to be a place for you—whether you’re passionate about the seed business or wish to work in an unparalleled team environment.

We’re always looking to add talented, passionate teammates. We’re about hard work, respect for our customers and each other, and building a company known for quality products and superior customer service. If you’re looking for a professional home and not just another job, we’d love to talk you about opportunities at Westar.
