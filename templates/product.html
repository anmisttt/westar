Description
An incredible producer of bright and vibrant red grape tomatoes. Very uniform and oblong, each individual tomato on this vine is bred to pack a delicious, sweet and savory taste. Very vigorous and will mature extremely early, pushing you first to market with the highest quality grape tomatoes, incomparable and competitive with the other products on the market in this segment. Vine is strong and indeterminate, fitting best for medium to higher technology greenhouse culture however it has adapted well to lower technology. Tommasino has a great disease package that includes resistance to many viruses such as fusarium, and much more. The yield on this variety is incredible, and the plants continue to yield even after multiple harvests. It is easily one of the best yielders on the market in this segment. Plant form makes it simple to harvest the tomatoes either individually or in clusters.
